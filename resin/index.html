About Our Process

Discover the artistry behind each piece

01

Design & Planning

Every piece begins with careful planning. We sketch designs, select color palettes, and prepare materials to ensure the perfect composition.

02

Material Preparation

We use only the highest quality resin, pigments, and additives. Each component is carefully measured and mixed to achieve the desired consistency and color.

03

Layering & Pouring

Our signature technique involves carefully layering different colored resins to create depth and movement. Each layer is poured with precision and timing.

04

Curing & Finishing

After pouring, each piece cures for 24-48 hours. We then sand, polish, and apply protective coatings to achieve a flawless, gallery-quality finish.
